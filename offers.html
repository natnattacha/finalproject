What We Offer

Meditation

Meditaion helps reduce stress and anxiety. It also improves focus and concentration, which would be especially helpful to students. You can choose how many minutes each session is, ranging from 3 minutes to 1 hour.

Daily Gratitude

To help increase positivity, we offer this Daily Gratitude journal. You can write down 3 things you are grateful for each day, and look back and reflect at the end of each month/year.

Motivational Podcasts

We offer various episodes of podcasts on the application. Each episode in a series will be about different areas in your life, so you can choose what you want to listen to. We are also inviting iconic figures in specific areas to be featured on our podcasts!
